Software

Mobile Application

The ImmerseMe VR platform features a user-friendly Android app for smartphones and tablets, allowing supervisors and staff to easily manage VR headsets for a seamless user experience.

Key Features and Benefits

  • Device Management: Select VR headsets from a list of detected devices, with real-time battery level and device name for reliable operation.
  • Real-Time Screencasting: Mirror the VR headset's display on the Android device with minimal latency, allowing supervisors to monitor and guide users.
  • Audio Control: Mute or unmute the VR headset's audio to ensure clear communication or a focused experience.
  • Recenter & Volume Adjustment: Remotely re-center the VR field of view and adjust volume for a comfortable, personalised experience.
  • Content Management: Organise and filter VR content, with controls to launch apps and manage 360° videos for easy navigation.
